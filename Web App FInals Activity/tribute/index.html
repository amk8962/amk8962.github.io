Isabel Malogdos

A woman who has more grandchildren than her age.

Here's how her life goes:

  • 1941 - Born in Tanulong, Besao Mountain Province
  • -She left her family in Besao and went to live with her husband in Sagada Mountain Province. But when the 4th child was born everything changed, her husband became abusive, she left him but he went to find them and brought them back home.
  • - Her 5th child dies due to a sickness that could not be cured due to lack of finances.
  • - Her husband suffered to a stroke which lead to him being paralyzed on half of his body. Being paralyzed and not being able to walk and word Mrs. Malogdos works hard to be able to support all her 9 children and her sick husband.
  • - 2 of her children graduated college and 1 of them went abroad and took her husband and children with her, the other one who graduated stayed in the Philippines and had a family of her own.
  • - All of her children got married at around 18 and 19 which may explain how she has more grandchildren than her age. She and grandpa took care of me when I was a baby, until I was in 1st grade of elementary.
